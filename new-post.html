Yesterday we commorated the 50th anniversary of man setting foot on the moon! The Soyuz MS-13 rocket launched yesterday, adding 3 more astronauts to the space station. What a special today to be blasted into space!

Also ^^^^ I did not know this, AMAZING! Buzz Aldrin is now my new hero :-) It wasn't recorded live because NASA was afraid of another lawsuit akin to when Genesis was read on Apollo 8th (O'Haire v. Paine). To be an astronaut would be a dream come true, to learn team skills, to be a scientist, and an engineer, how to speak Russian and so much more. I've seen astronauts say, leaving family behind is the hardest part of it all, knowing that maybe, maybe it could fail, even with the meticulous preparation, it's a serious career you have to be dedicated to. If your on Twitter I suggest giving a follow to https://twitter.com/Space_Station, you get a mix of everything.

SmarterEveryDay is one of my favorite Youtubers, I suggest you check out his channel! His one about grain bins was really good.

Also Mike Rowe has a really good show called Returning the Favor, free on Facebook, all of the family enjoys it!

One game I'm really enjoying, Sid Meier's Civilization VI. Usually it's CRAZY expensive, but it's 70% off right now making it doable. It's the most detailed game I've had a chance to play, it took a while but I'm finally starting to get the hang of it. I really enjoy picking the World Map as a random civilization, and you get placed wherever that civilization orginally started in real life.

A very simple total Civ VI beginners tutorial that helped me alot, they guy is engaging and keeps your attention the whole time, a very good teacher.

Just rewatched Big Hero 6 with my siblings. Such a good animated movie, it makes us break into laughter every time we watch it. I hope the sequel is just as good! Big Hero 7 will be released November 6th 2020
