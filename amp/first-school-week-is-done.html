We need a dog

By AnneMarie August 11, 2019

So busy last weekend (maybe just lazy) I never did my blog post! School went well, though it's always crazy getting into a rythm, I think mom was pleased with how it went, definitely enjoying our day of rest.

 Went to church and worshipped the Almighty God! We were invited to the Holters for lunch and met their 6 adorable puppies! Puppies worth $2,000+, they are labradoodles, all very sweet and quite adorable, luckily their teeth weren't in yet, otherwise my hands would be a sorry mess. 

Mom and I are trying to convince dad to get a dog, we had one a few years back that ended in diaster (had to put her done because she bit someone) We hope to get a Mastiff, we were looking at adoption options yesterday, there are a lot of good ones, but mom doesn't think they'd consider us for adoption since we wouldn't bring them to the vet that often. 

It's been pretty heated in politics this past week, especially gun control. Last week on Saturday the El Paso shooting claimed 22 lives, the youngest a 15 yr. old, just a little younger than Reuben. The Walmart was at it's full capacity between 2,000-3,000 people, police responded within 6 minutes. Victims of the shooting
