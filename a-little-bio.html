A little bio

AnneMarie

My name is Anne Marie, I'm homeschooled, I have been a Christian all my life, in all I do I hope to glorify my Creator!

I've lived in North Carolina my whole life, my mom is from Oregon, and my dad was born in Texas but moved when he was nine to NC. Oh and another thing I should mention is that I have 7 totally berserk (aka awesome) siblings running wild in the house! I wish I could have more siblings, but Dad is now counting on us for the next generation of Talbotts. My dad is a succesful entrepreneur, and one of the co-founders of Spreedly and was the CTO for quite a while. Just this year he left and is venturing into new business opportunities.
